# v0.1.0 Generated Asset Prompts

All project artwork was created with the built-in image-generation workflow.
No CLI fallback or API key was used.

## Estate overview

Create a wide, polished, realistic isometric Mediterranean vineyard estate
matching the approved Sunlit Terroir strategy-game interface. Include three
clearly distinct vineyard parcels, a central stucco winery, separate stone
cellar, bottling building, equipment shed, pale roads, and open space for
interactive overlays. Use warm morning light, sage foliage, terracotta roofs,
and parchment stone. No text, interface, logos, people, fantasy elements, or
watermark.

Saved as:
`public/packs/sunlit-terroir/images/estate-overview.png`

## Rival price war

Create a dark cinematic rival wine estate at blue hour, with an imposing stone
château on the right, vineyard foreground, warm windows, wine cases, and clean
negative space on the left for event choices. Use charcoal, midnight navy,
oxblood, and champagne light. No text, logo, face, television reference,
weapon, neon, or watermark.

Saved as:
`public/packs/sunlit-terroir/images/rival-price-war.png`

## Wine inventory

Create three individual premium fictional wine-bottle product images on a flat
warm parchment background: a dark reserve red with burgundy foil, a golden
Chardonnay with sage foil, and a pale salmon rosé with terracotta foil. Use
uncoated engraved labels without readable words or trademarks. Center each
bottle with generous padding and no props or watermark.

Saved as:

- `public/packs/sunlit-terroir/images/reserve-red.png`
- `public/packs/sunlit-terroir/images/estate-chardonnay.png`
